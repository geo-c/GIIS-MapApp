Natural Reserves Conservation Web Application

In the last decades, a lot of effort has been put into protecting natural reserves, and the species that inhabit those places, since society as a whole depends on their natural resources. This web application allows different stakeholders, to query and visualize important information related to each reserve, and the species that inhabit those places.

Choropleth

Dynamic choropleth visualization of reserve's attributes, such as biodiversity index (ratio of species by square kilometer) and deforestation index (percentage of deforestation on a yearly basis).

Species Information

Users can find information related to endangered and alien species, either by clicking each park polygon, or by using the searching bar. Further details about each animal are displayed in a pop-up.

Histograms

Dynamic histogram visualization of park's attributes. Users can personalize the threshold of the graph.
